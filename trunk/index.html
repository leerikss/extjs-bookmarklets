Translation Bookmarklet (Beta)

Installation

Translate  <<-- Drag this link into your bookmarks bar, or right click and Add to Favorites

Usage

Select text on any page, and click the "Translate" bookmark link.
The bookmarklet attempts to auto-detect the language from your selection, pops up a floating resizable drag & drop window, and translates the text into a language of your choice (for now defaults to Finnish). You can also manually copy & paste the text into the source textarea, and change the source and translation language.

Requirements

A valid browser; newest version of Chrome, Firefox, Opera, Safari, or IE 7+

Known issues

  • Depending on the CSS used on various pages, the bookmarklet might look a bit strange
  • Depending on the JavaScript used on various pages, the bookmarklet might not work as expected, or not work at all
  • Text selection might not work on all pages (ex. hotmail, gmail). In this case copy & paste the text manually
  • The bookmarklet loads dynamically quite an amount of resources (650 kB) from various locations. If some of the resources fail to load, the bookmarklet might look strange, or it might not work at all
  • IE: On some pages, won't work at all (ex. baidu.com)
